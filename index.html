Main Program

Lecture

Listen to speaker from various countries about the messages of sharing and opening. There will aslo be various tenor of the music industries whom will share their experiences and love for music

Bike Show

Bikers all around the world will be performing to celebrate the event. Professional bikers will held activities around every concert stage. Local bikers will be awarded and more...

Forum

Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with expert for each topic. People all around the world will discuss about rock music and the impact of rock culture in their society.

Workshop

Many workshops will be held all around the world for this special event to share and promote rock culture. Non-rock fans will have the opportunity to connect with rock culture, fans and rock-stars

Concert

Finally here comes what every one is waiting for. Rock concert all around the world at the same time. Each concert will begin with a tribute to Chester Bennington by local band
